## How to use Specs
Every significant feature must begin with a Spec that defines:
1. **Context**: Why are we making this change?
2. **Decision**: What exactly are we implementing?
3. **Consequences**: What are the trade-offs?

Use [template.md](./template.md) to draft new Specs.
